Welcome to my personal page !, I am {{ site.author }}, a PhD researcher specializing in Natural Language Processing, Semantic Representation, and Recommender System fields.
I conducted my PhD research at the LAB-STICC laboratory at IMT Atlantique, France, under the supervision of Professors Yannis Haralambous and Philippe Lenca. My doctoral research focused on the study of semantic representation for the French language, utilizing Combinatory Categorial Grammar and Discourse Representation Theory.
My current research focuses on applying advanced techniques in Natural Language Processing to enhance semantic representation and optimize recommender systems. I am particularly interested in exploring innovative approaches that leverage cutting-edge technologies to improve the understanding of linguistic nuances, enabling more accurate and context-aware semantic modeling. Through my work, I aim to contribute to the development of intelligent systems that can better comprehend and respond to the intricacies of human language, ultimately advancing the capabilities of recommender systems in various domains.
